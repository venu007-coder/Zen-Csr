About ZEN CSR

Zen CSR Ltd, based in High Wycombe, is developing a cloud-native construction project management platform tailored for construction firms.

It aims to streamline workflows, improve team communication, and boost project efficiency across all phases — from planning and tendering to budgeting, scheduling, and on-site collaboration.

Our Story

ZeN CSR started as provision of services to charities and found a niche that the bigger charities had property assets that required property management and thus created a new business unit to develop property management software to serve both charities and other businesses

Our solution also builds in compatibility with UK standards like Uniclass and BIM workflows, facilitating future integration with architectural and engineering models for clash detection and supply planning—all to reduce uncertainty, enhance safety, and minimise waste.


Our Mission & Vision

To develop a robust, UK-compliant construction management solution that streamlines operations, connects teams on and off-site, and enhances project outcomes through automation, mobility, and intelligent insights.

To transform the construction industry by delivering a unified, cloud-native platform that empowers firms to build smarter, faster, and more efficiently—driven by collaboration, real-time data, and sustainable practices.
